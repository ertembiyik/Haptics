1. Introduction

At Haptics, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Information: Includes name, email address, and other contact details provided by you directly when creating an account or contacting us.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access the app, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Data regarding your interactions with the app, including features used, in-app behavior, and preferences.
  • Location Data: If you allow us to access your location, we may collect information about your precise or approximate location as determined through GPS or other technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app’s services and features.
  • Personalize your experience and tailor content.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app’s performance and functionality.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and other app-related information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • With service providers who perform functions on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics) and are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to court orders or government requests.
  • To enforce our terms of service or protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our policies.

6. Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information. We encourage you to take steps to protect your own data by keeping your devices secure.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to correct any inaccuracies in your data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data under certain circumstances.
  • The right to restrict or object to the processing of your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
